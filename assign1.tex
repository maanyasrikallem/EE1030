$\cos{x} + \cos{2x} + ........ + \cos{nx} = \cos{\frac{n+1}{2}x}\sin{\frac{nx}{2}}\cosec{\frac{x}{2}}$
\hfill(1994-4 Marks)

\begin{align}
\binom{n}{m} +2\binom{n-1}{m} +3\binom{n-2}{m}+....+ \brak{n-m+1} \binom{m}{m} = \binom{n+2}{m+2}
